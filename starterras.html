How to Buy Pre-Terras

Follow these simple steps to purchase Pre-Terras and start earning from real estate and crypto yields.

1

Create a Wallet

Download MetaMask or your wallet of choice from the app store or Google Play Store for free. Desktop users can download the Google Chrome extension by going to metamask.io.

2

Get Some ETH

Have ETH in your wallet to swap for Pre-Terras. If you don't have any ETH, you can buy directly on MetaMask, transfer from another wallet, or buy on another exchange and send it to your wallet.

3

Go to Uniswap

Connect to Uniswap by going to app.uniswap.org in Google Chrome or on the browser inside your MetaMask app. Connect your wallet and paste the Pre-Terras contract address into Uniswap.

4

Swap ETH for Pre-Terras

Exchange your ETH for Pre-Terras. Set your slippage tolerance to 2-5% to ensure the transaction goes through smoothly. Confirm the transaction in your wallet when prompted.

5

Complete KYC

To receive full benefits and convert to the final Pre-Terras, complete our KYC process through our approved providers. This ensures compliance and unlocks all platform features.

6

Start Earning

Once you hold Pre-Terras, you'll automatically receive monthly yields from our diversified portfolio of real estate investments and crypto staking rewards directly to your wallet.

FAQs: Your Questions Answered

What makes Pre-Terras unique?
Pre-Terras uniquely combines crypto staking yields with real estate income through a leveraged model, providing dual appreciation and income streams.
What leverage level is used?
We target 1.25x to 2.5x leverage on initial capital using staked crypto as collateral.
What are the risks and how are they mitigated?
Risks include crypto volatility and real estate market fluctuations. Mitigated through diversification, hedging, NAV resets, and transparent reporting.
When are dividends/yields distributed?
Yields from rentals and staking are distributed monthly via the token directly to wallets.
How are NAV and capital gains calculated?
NAV tracks real estate value (monthly) and crypto treasury (daily), with gains from appreciation in both.

Yes, Pre-Terras offers liquidity for exits at any time through trading on supported platforms.
